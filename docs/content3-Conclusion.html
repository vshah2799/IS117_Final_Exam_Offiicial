Why Does It Matter and How to Be Prepared?

Previous Next

The internet will move to IPv6 and it will be sooner than you think. To make sure you do not get left behind in the trenches and start understanding the implications of the change all you have to do is invest in an IPv6 router. You don't have to get a fancy one, just one that supports the protocol. This will ensure you do not have to rush into changing your router and other electronics once the transition truly begins. Starting now will make the transition a lot easier and your new devices already support it so all you have to do is make one simple switch.
